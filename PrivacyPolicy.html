Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-02

TaskMate ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting user privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we use and store it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email, phone, profile photo, role (student/helper).
  • Post data: assignment details, class, subject, paper type, pages, offer price, attachments, location (if provided).
  • Messages: text and file exchanges in-app.
  • Usage data: device info, app usage, timestamps, analytics.
  • Payment data: transaction records; payment details handled by provider.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Operate TaskMate features: posting, matching, chat, delivery, wallet, payments.
  • Process payments, protect against fraud, manage escrow.
  • Send notifications (status, OTPs).
  • Improve services via analytics.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. Shared with payment processors, courier partners, service providers, or as required by law.

4. Data Retention & Security

Data is stored only as long as necessary. Reasonable safeguards are used. Report breaches to zsnapypro@gmail.com.

5. Your Controls

  • Update your profile in Account Settings.
  • Request account deletion (some data may remain for legal reasons).
  • Manage notification preferences in Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Parental supervision recommended for minors. Compliance with laws regarding minors' data.
