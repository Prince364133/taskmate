Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-02

TaskMate ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting user privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we use and store it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email, phone number, profile photo, role (student/helper).
  • Post data: assignment details, class, subject, paper type, pages, offer price, attachments and location (if provided for delivery).
  • Messages: text messages and files exchanged in-app for the purpose of completing work.
  • Usage data: device info, app usage events, timestamps to support delivery status, and analytics.
  • Payment data: transaction records. (Payment method details are processed by our payment provider and not stored in full on our servers.)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate TaskMate features (posting tasks, matching, chat, delivery OTP, wallet & payments).
  • To process payments, protect against fraud, and manage escrow releases.
  • To send transactional messages and notifications (e.g., order status, OTPs).
  • To improve our services via aggregated analytics and performance monitoring.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We share data where necessary to provide services: payment processors, courier partners (for physical delivery), service providers, and when required by law.

4. Data Retention & Security

We store data only as long as necessary for service operations and legal compliance. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect data. No system is 100% secure; report suspected breaches to zsnapypro@gmail.com.

5. Your Controls

  • Access & correction: You may update your profile details from Account Settings.
  • Delete account: You can request account deletion; some records required for legal reasons may be retained in limited form.
  • Notification preferences: Manageable in Settings inside the app.

6. Children's Privacy

TaskMate is intended for students and helpers. If a minor uses the service, parental consent and supervision are recommended. We will comply with applicable laws concerning minors' data.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, email us: zsnapypro@gmail.com.

Terms & Conditions

Effective date: 2025-10-02

Welcome to TaskMate. By using our services you agree to these Terms & Conditions. Please read carefully.

1. Service Overview

TaskMate is a platform connecting students (posters) with student-workers (helpers) who complete assignments and projects. TaskMate provides matching, messaging, escrow payments, and delivery tracking.

2. User Responsibilities

  • Posters must provide accurate task details and lawful, non-infringing content.
  • Helpers must perform work honestly and deliver in the agreed format and time.
  • Both parties will behave respectfully and follow academic integrity policies; TaskMate does not endorse academic dishonesty and reserves the right to suspend accounts that promote cheating.

3. Payments & Escrow

Posters pay upfront into an escrow. Funds are released to the helper upon successful delivery and confirmation (including OTP verification for in-person handoff). TaskMate may collect fees; detailed fee structure will be visible at checkout.

4. Delivery & Acceptance

Delivery may be digital (PDF) or physical (courier/in-person). Posters must inspect submissions and accept or request revisions within a defined review window.

5. Refunds & Disputes

Refunds are considered if work is not delivered or doesn't meet agreed requirements. Contact support at zsnapypro@gmail.com. TaskMate will assist but final resolution may require mutual agreement between parties.

6. Account Suspension & Termination

TaskMate may suspend or terminate accounts for violations (fraud, harassment, repeated low-quality work). Terminated accounts may not re-register without approval.

7. Limitation of Liability

To the extent permitted by law, TaskMate is not liable for indirect losses, lost profits, or academic penalties incurred as a result of using the platform.

8. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of India. Disputes should first attempt informal resolution via support; unresolved disputes follow legal procedures in the applicable jurisdiction.

9. Changes to Terms

We may update these Terms; we will notify users by email or in-app message. Continued use after update implies acceptance.

Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) — Summary

Last updated: 2025-10-02

This NDA summary sets expectations for confidentiality between Posters and Helpers on TaskMate. It is a concise overview (not a replacement for a signed legal NDA).

1. Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes any non-public details in task briefs, attachments, or communications labeled as confidential by a Poster.

2. Obligations

  • Helpers agree not to disclose, reproduce, or use Confidential Information for purposes beyond completing the assigned work.
  • Posters must mark confidential materials clearly and limit sharing to required parties.

3. Exceptions

Information already public, independently developed, or required to be disclosed by law is excluded from confidentiality obligations.

4. Breach & Remedies

Suspected breaches should be reported immediately to zsnapypro@gmail.com. Remedies may include account suspension, removal of content, or legal action.

To add a formal signed NDA for a particular job, parties should exchange a signed document outside the platform and notify TaskMate support.

About TaskMate

TaskMate is a student-first platform that connects students who need assignments, homework, or projects completed with peers who want to earn by doing those tasks. We focus on speed, convenience, and trust—by providing escrow payments, delivery verification, and an easy chat interface.

Mission

To empower students by reducing academic stress and creating fair earning opportunities for peers.

Vision

To be the go-to, trusted academic support platform for students everywhere.

Core Features

  • Post tasks with detailed specs (paper type, pen, pages, deadline).
  • Automatic matches by subject, class and location.
  • Secure escrow payments & OTP delivery verification.
  • In-app chat, PDF uploads, and delivery tracking.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a task?

From the Home screen tap "Post Work", fill the form with details (subject, pages, paper type, deadline), and confirm payment.

How are payments handled?

Payments go into escrow and are released when the Poster confirms delivery or when OTP verification is successful for in-person handoffs.

What if the helper doesn't deliver on time?

Open a dispute via the Support form. TaskMate will review chat logs, submissions and assist with resolution — refunds may be issued where appropriate.
